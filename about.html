About Us

Your Technology Partner Since 2001

Two decades of delivering enterprise-grade IT solutions to businesses across the UK.

Our Story

Founded in 2001, Sempervox began with a simple mission: to bring enterprise-level technology to small and medium-sized businesses at prices they could afford. What started as a small team in Leicester has grown into a trusted technology partner for organisations across the UK.

Over the past two decades, we've witnessed the digital transformation of British business firsthand. From the early days of dial-up internet to today's cloud-first world, we've helped our clients navigate every technological shift, ensuring they always have the tools they need to succeed.

Today, Sempervox continues to operate from our Leicester headquarters, serving clients nationwide with the same commitment to quality and personal service that defined us from day one.
